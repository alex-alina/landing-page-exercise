Origami Home Decorations

Quality Origami Paper

Our basic paper supply comes from the top origami paper producers. Most of it comes directly from Japan, but we also work with the best European producers of special paper.

Attention to Detail

Each fold is carefully and precisely made and each componet is flawlessly placed in the final ensemble. Each final decoration is unique and placed inside a glass container that adds elegance and protects it against dust.

Careful Packaging and Delivery

Our decorations are beautiful but fragile. Because of that we protect them during the delivery process by wrapping them in custom made packages. As a result each decoration will reach its destination undamaged.
